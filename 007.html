Cycle Time & Flow Rate (cont)

 

  • That's a fair amount of oil to pump -- think about that the next time you watch how quickly a hydraulic backhoe or skid/loader is able to move!
  • In our log splitter, the maximum flow rate is 11 gallons per minute.
  • That means that it will take 10 or so seconds to draw the piston back after the log is split, and it may take almost 30 seconds to push the piston through a tough log (because the flow rate is lower at high pressures).
