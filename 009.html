CALCULATIONS

Piston Area:

piston diameter = 4 in.

Area = π*d^2/4(in.^2)

Area = π*4^2/4(in.^2) = 12.57(in.^2)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Force of Cylinder based on a Maximum Pressure = 3,000 psi:

Force (lbs) = Pressure (psi) * Area (in.^2)

Force (lbs) = 3000 psi * 12.57(in.^2) = 37,710 lbs

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Pressure if the Log-Splitter could deliver a Force of 20 tons:

Pressure = 40,000 (lbs) /  12.57(in.^2) = 3182.18 psi

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

% Error or Difference between Measured and Expected Pressures:

%error = (Measured - Expected) / Expected * 100%

%error = (3000 - 3182.18) / 3182.18 * 100% = -5.73%
