The Real World

 

  • One thing you can see is that the advertised "20-ton splitting force" is generous.
  • A 4-inch piston has an area of 12.56 square inches.
  •  If the pump generates a maximum pressure of 3,000 pounds per square inch (psi), the total pressure available is 37,680 pounds, or about 2,320 pounds shy of 20 tons. Oh well...
